    Monica Geller

    Member of the week

    Monica Geller is a Junior here at the University of North Georgia, Dahlonega She is form Augusta, Georgia and is a Computer Science major with a Minor in business. Among being a member in The UNG Computer Science Club she is also a member of Delta Kappa Kappa honors fraternity, the UNG Recycling Club, and a frequent volunteer at the Dahlonega Animal Shelter. Monica was one of the members on the competing team who lead our school to a victorious 4th place finish at for the National Coding Competition. Monica says that "being part of the Computer Science club not only gives me numerous opportunities for advancing my knowledge of Computer Science, but has also given me a sense of community since coming to UNG". Monica's contribution to our club is great and we are proud to have this NightHawk.
