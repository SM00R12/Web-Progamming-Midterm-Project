    Hacker Halted

    This Thursday and Friday,October 10th and 11th, will be this year's Hacker Halted event. Hacker Halted is free to all students who have a valid UNG ID. Attending is also an excused abscence from all classes on Thursday and Friday, so please attend! This event is a great way to learn more about the world of Cybersecurity. Email our secretary Pheobe Buffay if you have any questions. Hope to see everyone there!

    Who we are.

    Founded in 2017, the Computer Science Club at UNG Dahlonega is a University sponsored organization dedicated to educating and empowering students who are eager to learn more about Computer Science and Cybersecurity. We participate in multiple events throughout the school year and aide in the profesional and academic development of our members through weekly hands-on learning based activies. We strive to create a community of like-minded indviduals and provide them with career builiding opporunities.
